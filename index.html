Brief Biography 

He Yan received his B.S. degree in College of Information Science and Technology from Nanjing Forestry University in 2014. From 2017, he is a Ph.D Student of School of Computer Science and Engineering in Nanjing University of Science and Technology and a member of Pattern Recognition and Bioinformatics Group, led by professor Dong-Jun Yu.
